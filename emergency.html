In an Emergency

If you suspect a heart attack, take immediate action. Call the emergency number for your region and follow the steps below.

Select your region:

United Kingdom

Dial 999 immediately.

If you are not allergic and have no contraindications, chew one 300 mg aspirin tablet while waiting for the ambulance. Always follow local medical advice.

United States & Canada

Dial 911 immediately.

The emergency operator may advise taking aspirin (160–325 mg chewable) if appropriate. Do not self-administer unless instructed.

European Union

Dial 112 immediately.

Guidance varies by country. Contact emergency services first; they may advise on aspirin use.
