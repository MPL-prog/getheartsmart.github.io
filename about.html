About GetHeartSmart

GetHeartSmart is a public‑information project designed to help people recognise the signs of a heart attack, act quickly and access trustworthy guidance. We believe that clear, calm information can save lives.

Our purpose

We provide region‑specific emergency numbers, explain common heart attack symptoms, and point to reputable organisations for further information. Our goal is to empower the general public, carers and first responders with actionable knowledge.

Our values

  • Credibility: We reference established public health resources such as the NHS, American Heart Association and European Society of Cardiology.
  • Clarity: We use plain language, avoiding jargon and hype. Our content is reviewed to ensure it remains non‑diagnostic and non‑prescriptive.
  • Accessibility: Our website is designed for good contrast, simple navigation and responsiveness on mobile devices. We continuously work to improve accessibility.
  • Non‑commercial: We do not sell products or promote specific treatments. We simply aim to support rapid recognition and response.

Limitations

This site does not provide medical advice, diagnosis or treatment. For personal health concerns, please consult a qualified healthcare professional. In an emergency, always call your local emergency number immediately.
